What's the difference between business and personal?

The personal license can be used at work or at home with your home-lab. With the personal use license, you can only deploy one tunnel, but the business edition unlocks commercial support and other restrictions on use.

You can use your license for any kind of development. However, only you may use your personal license; you may not share it with others. Your company cannot pay, reimburse, or in any way finance your personal license.

What is acceptable use for a personal license? Anything that you as an individual may do in your daily workflow to achieve your job as an individual. In other words, this license should not be used by other people in your company, or as part of a company system or permanent integration.

  • Sharing work with a client.
  • Developing a plugin or API integration, and needing to receive webhoooks.
  • Exposing a self-hosted service from a Kubernetes cluster for development and testing.
  • For use on your home network or Raspberry Pi to expose services or link networks.
  • Evaluating inlets PRO before purchasing a business license.
